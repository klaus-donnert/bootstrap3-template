What did we do?

Colormind.io

We used the Colormind AI to extract our color palette from the above image. It's easy to use. Give it a try. Then we used that palette in custom.css after our already customized bootstrap.css. We also used lots of dummy text - in case you haven't already noticed. As an aside, did you know that the whole point of using Lorem Ipsum is to show you normal looking text with normal distribution, without distracting you into reading the pseudo content so that you can pay attention to the layout instead. I just thought it was interesting.

Other Thoughts

Some of the links and buttons, and all of the forms, on these pages do not work. That is intentional. This is a just an exercise using Bootstrap 3. Nothing more.
